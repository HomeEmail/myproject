暨南大学是中国第一所招收外国留学生的大学，也是目前全国境外学生最多的大学；至今，共培养了160个国家的各类人才20余万人，已同248所海外高等院校和文化机构签订了双边协议或建立了学术交流关系。

Our program is held in cooperation with Jinan niversity, located in the Tianhe District, Guangzhou. Jinan University is the first university in China to accept foreign students from all parts of the world in addition to hosting the largest number of foreign students in China. To date, more than 20,000 talented individuals from over 160 countries have graduated from this university. Furthermore, the university cooperates with 248 other overseas higher education and cultural institutions.

卓越教育成立于1997年，是全国领先、华南地区规模最大、质量最好的中小学课外教育机构。她拥有一支逾4000名优秀教师的师资队伍，目前全国有160多间校区，每年就读的学生超过40万人次，培养的优秀学子遍布世界各地。

Bestudy Education Group, founded in 1997, is the top most influential educational organization in southern China that offers primary and secondary education. Due to the superb qualifications and training of our teachers, amounting to more than 4000, we have expanded over the years to 160 school districts and have accepted more than 40,000 students a year.

暨大•卓越国际高中由卓越教育与暨南大学合作办学，引进美国高中一流的教学体系和教学理念，在保证普通高中合格毕业的同时，着重培养学生国际化的视野和学术技能、自主学习能力、批判思维能力，借助于暨南大学丰富的国际资源和卓越教育优秀的国内国外师资，经过三年学习，学生能够顺利进入世界优秀大学。

ZYIE International Program at Jinan University is the product of cooperation between Bestudy Education Group and Jinan University. Our school reflects and emphasizes American educational beliefs and methods and has the ultimate goal of preparing students for admission into top ranking US universities. After three years’ study in our school, students will have gained global curiosity, learned to take the initiative, acquired critical thinking skills.
